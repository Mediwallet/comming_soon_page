Diagnostic Portal

  • Purpose: Facilitate the management and delivery of diagnostic services, including lab tests and imaging studies, for both healthcare providers and patients.
    • Key Features:
  • Test Ordering: Allow healthcare providers to electronically order lab tests and imaging studies.
  • Sample Tracking: Track the status of samples from collection to analysis.
  • Results Reporting: Securely deliver test results and imaging reports to ordering physicians and patients (through the patient portal).
  • Image Viewing (for Imaging): Provide authorized users with access to view medical images.
  • Integration with Lab Information Systems (LIS) and Radiology Information Systems (RIS): Seamless data exchange with diagnostic equipment and systems.
  • Billing & Invoicing: Manage billing for diagnostic services.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Generate reports on test volumes, turnaround times, and other relevant metrics.
    • Benefits:
  • Faster turnaround times for diagnostic results.
  • Improved accuracy and reduced errors in test ordering and reporting.
  • Enhanced communication between diagnostic facilities and referring physicians.
  • Convenient access for patients to their diagnostic reports.
  • Streamlined workflows for diagnostic centers.
