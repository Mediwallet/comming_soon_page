Patient Portal

  • Purpose:Empower patients with secure access to their health information and facilitate communication with their healthcare providers.
    • Key Features:
  • View Medical Records: Access to lab results, medications, allergies, immunizations, past visit summaries, and discharge summaries.
  • Appointment Management: Request, schedule, reschedule, and cancel appointments. View upcoming and past appointments.
  • Prescription Refills: Request medication refills online.
  • Secure Messaging: Communicate with doctors, nurses, and other healthcare staff through secure messaging.
  • Bill Payment: View and pay medical bills online. Access billing statements and payment history.
  • Personal Health Information: Update contact information, insurance details, and emergency contacts.
  • Health Tracking: Option for patients to track their vital signs, symptoms, and other health-related data (if integrated).
  • Educational Resources: Access to relevant health information and educational materials.
    • Benefits:
  • Improved patient engagement and adherence to treatment plans.
  • Increased convenience and reduced phone calls for administrative tasks.
  • Enhanced communication with healthcare providers.
  • Better access to personal health information, leading to informed decision-making.
  • Streamlined billing and payment processes.
