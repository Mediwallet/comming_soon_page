PHR Benefits:

Indeed! PHR has a number of advantages for both patients and healthcare providers. For example, it aids clinicians while simultaneously allowing patients to submit their data to clinicians.

  • 1. It monitors and evaluates your health. It keeps track of your progress toward your health goals and aids with cholesterol reduction.

  • 2. It analyses data from home-monitoring devices and schedules doctor appointments for you.

  • 3. It keeps track of vaccinations and appointments, as well as organising your therapy. According to research, when parents use PHR for their children, they are more likely to receive better treatment.

  • 4. It increases patient participation by allowing them to see all of their health information. It is easier for patients to chose better treatment when they can follow their health status.

  • 5. It promotes better health by assisting patients in coordinating information from many sources. It also ensures that patient information is accessible via the internet.

  • 6. Using a PHR with quick access to electronic prescriptions and scheduling software, the healthcare institution can save money on administrative costs.
